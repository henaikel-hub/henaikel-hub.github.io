Seleccion Time

The football watch face for Apple Watch

Turn your Apple Watch into a football clock. Choose from 48 national soccer balls, each a beautifully designed analog watch face — read the time off a crisp dial, or tap to hide the hands and admire the ball art.

  • 48 nations, each a unique ball
  • Live analog clock with a red second hand
  • Tap to hide the clock for pure ball art
  • Swipe to switch countries & auto-shuffle
  • Free to start — Collector Pass unlocks all
